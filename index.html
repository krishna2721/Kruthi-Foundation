About Us

Kruthi Foundation is a charitable organization dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged communities. Our focus is on education, healthcare support and community development initiatives that bring sustainable change.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals through education, social support and opportunity. We believe that every person deserves access to resources that help them build a better future.
